Role Profile: Maintenance Technician
Department: Production
======================================

1. Primary Objective
To ensure all manufacturing equipment and machinery are operating safely and efficiently by performing preventive maintenance and timely repairs, minimizing production downtime.

2. Key Responsibilities
- Perform scheduled preventive maintenance on all production machinery according to the schedule in the Odoo Maintenance module.
- Respond promptly to equipment breakdown requests logged by Production Operators.
- Diagnose mechanical and electrical faults in machinery.
- Repair and replace defective parts.
- Maintain a detailed log of all maintenance and repair activities for each piece of equipment in Odoo.
- Manage the inventory of spare parts and request new parts as needed.
- Adhere to all safety protocols, especially during machine repairs.

3. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Equipment Downtime (%).
- Mean Time Between Failures (MTBF).
- Mean Time to Repair (MTTR).
- Preventive Maintenance compliance rate (%).
- Spare parts inventory accuracy.

4. Core Workflows & Tools
- Your primary workspace is the **Odoo Maintenance** module, where you will manage "Maintenance Requests" and "Preventive Maintenance" schedules.
- You will use a variety of mechanical and electrical tools.
- You must follow all safety procedures, including Lockout/Tagout (LOTO) when working on machinery.

5. Collaboration
- Reports to the Production Supervisor or a Maintenance Lead.
- Works closely with Production Operators to understand equipment issues and schedule maintenance.
- Coordinates with the Procurement department to order spare parts.
