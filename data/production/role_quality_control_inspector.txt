Role Profile: Quality Control Inspector
Department: Production
======================================

1. Primary Objective
To ensure that all raw materials, in-process products, and finished goods meet the established quality standards and specifications set by WellaWorX.

2. Key Responsibilities
- Perform inspections on incoming raw materials and components to ensure they meet quality requirements.
- Conduct in-process quality checks at critical stages of the manufacturing process, as defined in the Odoo Quality module.
- Perform final inspections on finished products before they are transferred to the warehouse.
- Accurately record all inspection results, measurements, and test data in the Odoo Quality module.
- Investigate the root cause of quality issues when a product fails a check and collaborate on corrective actions.
- Calibrate and maintain inspection and testing equipment.
- Help develop and refine Standard Operating Procedures (SOPs) for quality control.

3. KPIs (Key Performance Indicators)
- Defect rate of finished products (%).
- In-process inspection pass/fail rate.
- Timeliness of inspection and reporting.
- Reduction in customer complaints related to product quality.
- Accuracy of quality documentation.

4. Tools
- Your primary tool is the **Odoo Quality** module, where you will manage "Quality Checks" and "Quality Alerts".
- You must be an expert in the procedures outlined in the **"quality_control_sop.txt"** document.
- You will use various physical measurement tools (e.g., calipers, micrometers, gauges) in your daily work.

5. Collaboration
- Reports to the Production Supervisor or a dedicated Quality Manager.
- Works very closely with Production Operators to provide real-time feedback.
- Collaborates with the Procurement team on raw material quality and vendor issues.
