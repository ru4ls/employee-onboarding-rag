Role Profile: IT Support Specialist
Department: IT
======================================

1. Primary Objective
To provide timely and effective technical support to all WellaWorX employees, ensuring the smooth operation of all IT systems, hardware, and software.

2. Key Responsibilities
- Serve as the first point of contact for employees seeking technical assistance.
- Troubleshoot and resolve issues with hardware (laptops, printers), software (Odoo, MS Office), and network connectivity.
- Set up and configure new user accounts and equipment.
- Manage user access permissions in Odoo and other systems as directed.
- Escalate unresolved issues to the System Administrator or other senior IT staff.
- Educate users on IT best practices and security policies.

3. KPIs (Key Performance Indicators)
- First-contact resolution rate (%).
- Average ticket resolution time.
- User satisfaction score (CSAT) from support tickets.
- Adherence to Service Level Agreements (SLAs).

4. Tools
- You will primarily work within the **Odoo Helpdesk module** to manage support tickets.
- You must be an expert on the procedures outlined in all "how-to" guides (e.g., "how_to_connect_vpn.txt", "how_to_reset_password.txt").
- You will enforce the policies outlined in "company_security_guidelines.txt".

5. Collaboration
- Reports to the IT Manager/System Administrator.
- Interacts with all employees across all departments.
