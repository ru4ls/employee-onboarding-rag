Role Profile: Logistics Coordinator
Department: Warehouse
======================================

1. Primary Objective
To manage and coordinate the transportation of goods, overseeing all incoming and outgoing shipments to ensure they are timely, cost-effective, and compliant with all regulations.

2. Key Responsibilities
- Plan and schedule all inbound and outbound shipments, coordinating with freight carriers and transport companies.
- Prepare and manage all shipping documentation, including packing lists, bills of lading, and customs forms if applicable.
- Track shipments in transit to ensure on-time delivery and communicate any delays to relevant stakeholders (e.g., Sales, Customer Service).
- Optimize shipping routes and methods to reduce transportation costs.
- Act as the main point of contact for resolving any shipment issues, such as wrong deliveries or goods damaged in transit.
- Verify and approve freight invoices for payment by the Finance department.

3. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- On-Time Delivery (OTD) rate (%).
- Freight cost per unit or per shipment.
- Accuracy of shipping documentation.
- Reduction in shipping errors and delays.

4. Core Workflows & Tools
- You will primarily use the Odoo Inventory module to manage "Delivery Orders" and "Receipts".
- You may use external carrier portals and software to book and track shipments.
- Strong communication via email and phone is essential for this role.

5. Collaboration
- Reports to the Warehouse Supervisor or a Logistics Manager.
- Coordinates with Warehouse Operators to ensure orders are packed and ready for dispatch.
- Works closely with the Sales and Customer Service teams to provide shipping updates.
- Interacts with the Finance department regarding freight billing.
