Role Profile: Marketing Associate
Department: Marketing
======================================

1. Primary Objective
To support the Marketing team in the execution of campaigns and daily activities, helping to increase brand awareness and generate leads.

2. Key Responsibilities
- Assist in creating and scheduling content for social media channels using the Odoo Social Marketing module.
- Help draft copy for email newsletters and promotional materials.
- Assist the Campaign Manager with logistics for events and webinars.
- Gather data and metrics for weekly campaign performance reports.
- Ensure all created materials adhere to the company's brand guidelines.

3. KPIs (Key Performance Indicators)
- Timeliness of task completion.
- Engagement rate on social media posts you manage.
- Quality and accuracy of data gathered for reports.

4. Tools
- You will use Odoo's **Email Marketing** and **Social Marketing** apps.
- Familiarize yourself with our design tools like Canva.
- All work must follow the rules in the "Brand Guidelines" section of the "marketing_onboarding.txt" guide.

5. Collaboration
- Reports to the Marketing Lead.
- Works closely with the Digital Content Specialist and Campaign Manager.
