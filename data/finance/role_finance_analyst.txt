Role Profile: Finance Analyst
Department: Finance
======================================

1. Primary Objective
To analyze financial data, prepare insightful reports, and support the company's budgeting and forecasting processes to aid in strategic decision-making.

2. Key Responsibilities
- Prepare and analyze monthly, quarterly, and annual financial performance reports.
- Develop financial models and conduct variance analysis (Budget vs. Actual).
- Assist the Finance Manager in the annual budget creation process and periodic forecasting.
- Monitor key financial performance indicators (KPIs) and report on trends.
- Provide data-driven insights and recommendations to support business strategy.
- Conduct ad-hoc financial analysis as requested by management.

3. KPIs (Key Performance Indicators)
- Accuracy of financial forecasts (Forecast vs. Actual %).
- Timeliness and clarity of financial reports.
- Quality of insights and recommendations provided.
- Efficiency in data modeling and analysis.

4. Tools
- You will heavily use the **Reporting** features within the **Odoo Accounting module** to extract raw data.
- **Excel/Google Sheets** are your primary tools for detailed analysis, modeling, and creating management-ready reports.
- You must have a deep understanding of the company's financial structure and chart of accounts in Odoo.

5. Collaboration
- Reports to the Finance Manager.
- Works closely with all members of the Finance team and provides analytical support to other department heads during budget cycles.
