Role Profile: AP/AR Officer
Department: Finance
======================================

1. Primary Objective
To manage the full lifecycle of accounts payable (AP) and accounts receivable (AR), ensuring timely payments to vendors and prompt collections from customers.

2. Key Responsibilities
- **Accounts Receivable (AR):**
  - Generate and send out customer invoices from Odoo.
  - Monitor customer accounts for overdue payments.
  - Follow up on outstanding invoices via email and phone.
  - Collaborate with the Sales department to resolve billing discrepancies.
- **Accounts Payable (AP):**
  - Process and record incoming vendor bills in Odoo.
  - Match vendor bills against purchase orders and receipts.
  - Schedule and prepare weekly payment runs for approval by the Finance Manager.

3. Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
- Days Sales Outstanding (DSO) for AR.
- Invoice processing time (AP).
- Collection effectiveness rate (%).
- Accuracy of payment processing.

4. Core Workflows & Tools
- You will primarily use the "Customer Invoices" and "Vendor Bills" sections within the **Odoo Accounting module**.
- You must be proficient in the company's invoicing and collection procedures outlined in "finance_onboarding.txt".

5. Collaboration
- Reports to the Finance Manager.
- Works with the Sales team on customer billing and the Procurement team on vendor payments.
